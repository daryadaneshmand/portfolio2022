AI Art Generation for Journalism (In Progress)

Model Training, Product Development

Project Description

Knight Lab Studio is a by-application course through which cross-disciplinary teams collaborate to create cutting-edge digital work, combining research, design thinking, and analytical methods.

Over 10 weeks, the 10-person AI Art Generation project team will work to produce and build upon a method for turning an article into a stable diffusion text prompt, returning AI generated art recommendations to go with the article.

Role

My focus within this project is on retraining the current model while collaborating and sharing findings with teamates focusing on social media experimentation and automation. Our training goals includes correcting for key issues including Western and racial biases and copyright.
